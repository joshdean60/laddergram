Privacy Policy

Laddergram · Last updated June 15, 2026

Laddergram ("the app") is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains what information the app and its advertising partner do and do not handle.

We Do Not Collect Personal Data

The developer of Laddergram does not collect, store on our own servers, or sell any personal information about you. The app has no developer accounts and no developer-run analytics.

Your Game Data Stays on Your Device

All app data — your daily progress, puzzle history, streaks, statistics, and difficulty preference — is stored locally on your device only. It never leaves your phone to reach us, and removing the app deletes it.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Laddergram is free and supported by ads. The app displays occasional full-screen (interstitial) ads using Google AdMob. To serve and measure these ads, Google and its ad partners may collect and process certain information from your device, which may include:

  • Device and mobile advertising identifiers
  • IP address (used for coarse, region-level location and fraud prevention)
  • Device type, operating system, and similar technical/diagnostic information
  • Ad interaction data (such as ad views and taps)

We configure AdMob to request non-personalized ads only. That means ads are not targeted based on a profile of your interests, and the app does not track you across other apps and websites. Because of this, the app does not present an App Tracking Transparency prompt. Ad content is limited to a general (all-ages) rating.

Data handled for advertising is collected by Google as an independent party, in line with Google's own policies. To learn how Google uses this information, see Google's Privacy & Terms and How Google uses data when you use our partners' apps.

No Accounts

The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide an email address, name, or other identifying information.

Children's Privacy

Laddergram is rated for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, ads are non-personalized, and ad content is restricted to a general audience rating.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
