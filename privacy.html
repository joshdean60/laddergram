Privacy Policy

Laddergram · Last updated June 15, 2026

Laddergram ("the app") is designed to respect your privacy completely. This policy explains what information the app does and does not handle.

We Do Not Collect Any Data

Laddergram does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. We have no servers that receive information about you or your use of the app.

Everything Stays on Your Device

All app data — your daily progress, puzzle history, streaks, statistics, and difficulty preference — is stored locally on your device only. It never leaves your phone and is not backed up to us. Removing the app deletes this data.

No Accounts

The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide an email address, name, or any other identifying information.

No Advertising

Laddergram contains no advertisements and no advertising identifiers (no IDFA). We do not use any ad networks.

No Third-Party Tracking or Analytics

The app includes no third-party analytics, tracking, or measurement tools. No usage data is sent to any third party.

Children's Privacy

Because the app collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children. Laddergram is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
